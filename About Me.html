About Me

Shiv Kanade: An Aspiring Engineer and a Drone Fanatic


Hello there! My name is Shiv Kanade, and I'm a high school student in grade 11 enrolled in the MaCS Program. My interests lie in the STEM fields, particularly engineering. Apart from academics, I indulge in various hobbies to continuously enrich my skills. Some of these hobbies include 3D printing and Blender modeling, as these skills allow me to express my ideas and bring them to life. Reading novels is another passion of mine as it helps me develop my critical thinking and communication skills. I also enjoy skiing as it allows me to connect with nature and provides a perfect balance of physical activity and fun. Having a diverse set of hobbies is crucial as it enables me to maintain a balanced and healthy lifestyle.


Beyond my academic studies, I have a strong interest in extracurricular activities. I participate in my school's robotics team, where I apply my critical thinking and problem-solving skills to overcome challenges in project development. Alongside, I also collaborate with my team at my school's Engineering Club (MEC) in order to develop exciting and enriching weekly events, such as the Model Rocket competition we ran! Additionally, I volunteer regularly at the local community center, where I organize events for children in my neighborhood. I firmly believe that engaging in both school and community activities is essential for personal growth and development, and I strive to make a positive impact wherever I can.



One of the things that I am most proud of is my dedication to learning and self-improvement. I am constantly seeking out new challenges and opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills. Whether it's through online courses, workshops, or personal projects, I am always looking for ways to push myself to be the best that I can be. I believe that a strong work ethic and a growth mindset are essential for achieving success in any field, and I strive to embody these qualities in everything that I do.
